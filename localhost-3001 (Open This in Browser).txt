
  ========================================================

     YOUR APP URL:  http://localhost:5173

  ========================================================

  How to open GST Billing Software:

  1. Copy this URL:  http://localhost:5173
  2. Paste it in your browser (Chrome, Edge, or any browser)
  3. Press Enter

  That's it! Your billing app will open.


  --------------------------------------------------------
  PAGE NOT LOADING?
  --------------------------------------------------------

  If you see an error or blank page, the local server
  is not running. To start it:

  1. Double-click "start-fmnBilling.bat" in this folder

  2. Wait a few seconds for it to start

  3. Now open http://localhost:5173 in your browser


  --------------------------------------------------------
  WHAT IS LOCALHOST?
  --------------------------------------------------------

  "localhost" means YOUR computer. This app runs entirely
  on your machine — nothing is sent to the internet.

  - Your invoices = stored on YOUR computer
  - Your client data = stored on YOUR computer
  - Your business info = stored on YOUR computer

  No cloud. No signup. No one else can see your data.


  --------------------------------------------------------
  TIP: INSTALL AS DESKTOP APP
  --------------------------------------------------------

  So you don't need to type this URL every time:

  1. Open http://localhost:5173 in Chrome or Edge
  2. Click the blue "Install App" bar at the top
  3. Done! Now it works like a normal app on your Desktop
